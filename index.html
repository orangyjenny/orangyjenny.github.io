Hey, I'm W

or call me Jenny, Jen, orangy, etc ;)

I'm a self-taught digital artist constantly learning new stuff trying to improve my skills everyday. I always love to try new things so I don't have consistency in style at all. I just started from June 2020.

The majority of drawings that I do is semi-realistic figure drawing/portraits. This includes fan arts or original works. I'm trying to work harder on background to create illustrations with more finished looking. I also work on character design occassionaly.

Fan arts that I have done by far are mostly about JJBA and Hetalia. Fav in JJBA is Bucciarati and fav in Hetalia is Lovino. But I do plan to draw fan arts of other anime.

I'm most active on twitter Welcome to talk to me on twitter if you want to :)
